Privacy Policy for Kryptex

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

🚀 TL;DR (Too Long; Didn't Read)

  • Zero-Knowledge: We use client-side AES-256 encryption. We cannot see your files, messages, or passwords.
  • On-Device Processing: Financial SMS parsing happens locally. No raw transaction data is uploaded.
  • Login Only: We use OAuth 2.0 (Google, X, MS, Yahoo) only for identity. We do not read your emails or tweets.
  • Data Security: Your data is stored as encrypted blobs. Even the storage provider (MEGA) cannot read it.
  • No Selling: We do not sell your data or use it for AI/ML training.

1. Introduction

Kryptex is built on the principle of Privacy by Design. As a Zero-Knowledge Super App, we have architected the system so that we have a technical inability to access your unencrypted data. Our policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and the Indian Information Technology Act.

The Zero-Knowledge Guarantee: Your Master Password is the ONLY key. If lost, Kryptex staff cannot recover your data.

2. Encryption Architecture

All sensitive data (Digital Vault, Finance Manager, Smart Assistant notes) is encrypted on your device using AES-256 before being transmitted to any server.

3. Data Collection and Usage

3.1 Authentication (OAuth 2.0)

We use OAuth 2.0 via Google, Microsoft, X (Twitter), and Yahoo for identity verification. We only retrieve basic identity metadata (Email, Name, Unique Provider ID) to create your account.

3.2 Financial Privacy (Local SMS Parsing)

The Finance Engine tracks expenses via SMS. All parsing occurs strictly on-device. Raw SMS data is never uploaded to our servers.

4. Storage & Infrastructure

Encrypted file "blobs" are stored via the MEGA API. Metadata is managed via a Bitwarden-compatible architecture. We use Redis for temporary, encrypted session caching.

5. No Data Monetization

We do not sell data to third parties, nor do we use your encrypted data for AI model training.

6. Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of Hyderabad, India.
