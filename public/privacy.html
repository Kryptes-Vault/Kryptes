Privacy Policy for Kryptex

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Kryptex is designed so that only you can access your data. We use client-side AES-256 encryption before your data is stored or transmitted. We have no access to your master password, recovery keys, or vault contents.

1. Information We Collect

We use OAuth 2.0 (Google, X, Microsoft, Yahoo) for account creation and identity verification. We only collect your email and name to distinguish your account.

2. Local Data Processing

All sensitive information, including SMS parsing for financial tracking and document management, occurs locally on your device. Only encrypted blobs are stored in the cloud.

3. Encrypted Storage

Metadata is stored in a secure database as encrypted records. Large files are handled via the MEGA API, where they are stored as encrypted blobs that neither Kryptex nor MEGA can access.

4. Your Responsibility

The Master Password and Recovery Key are your sole responsibility. If lost, your data is permanently unrecoverable.

5. Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of Hyderabad, India.
